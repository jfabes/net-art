"SCANDALISHIOUS" by Ann Hirsch

Year: 2008-2009

This artwork narrates the tale of a young girl thrust into the spotlight of YouTube, facing the scrutiny of thousands of strangers in a platform that was once unfamiliar to many. Through her videos, crafted to challenge her audience's perceptions of normalcy, she inadvertently redefined the concept of confidence. The reactions to her experiments unveil the story of the anonymous viewer and their undeniable influence in shaping her treatment, driven by their own preconceived notions and biases regarding women. It's a story that is currently well known and debated heavily, but for her it was new and groundbreaking.
