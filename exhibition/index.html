Net-art is Worth a Billion Pixels

Exhibition curated by Juliette Faber

The act of storytelling transcends age, sex, and time, connecting all who partake. Great storytelling utilizes humanity's urge to connect to its advantage. The phrase, "A picture is worth million words", has taken recent society by storm. The expansion of technology leaves many stories to be told on an expansive scale. Now, more than ever, we have the opportunity to document and tell even the most mundane stories. From the humanization of dehumanized cam girls on the internet over an 18-month journey, to the moments captured on Google Images telling a higher level story of humanity, to the exploration of digital narrative possibilities. The internet has opened a new can of worms that has raised questions about humanity's morals and its ability to connect through the electricity that binds us all.
