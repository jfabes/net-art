"Nine Eyes of Google Street View" by Jon Rafman

Year: 2008 - Ongoing

This ongoing net art project features photographs taken three years ago, each captured by Google's camera, depicting the mundane yet profound experiences of humanity. These images encapsulate moments of altruism, nurturing, and the unbridled expression of innate emotions. Together, they offer a poignant glimpse into the collective narrative of humanity.
